# Living Lunar Intelligence

Dialuna is a personal rhythm operating system, not a period dashboard. Cycle, energy, mood, symptoms, and guidance behave as one living field that changes with the user.

## Product promise

- Make today's body state understandable in seconds.
- Turn repeated check-ins into calm, actionable pattern recognition.
- Feel feminine without pink wellness cliches, futuristic without sci-fi chrome, and premium without ornamental luxury.
- Preserve every current business rule, route, persisted value, and localization contract.

## Signature

The memorable object is the **Rhythm Field**: an organic, layered signal made from phase arc, density, focal light, and contextual type. The memorable interaction is continuity: the field transforms when a day, phase, check-in, or destination changes.
